WHAT WE DO?
 NeighShop, our startup, is a dynamic online marketplace designed to empower local sellers and connect communities. With a diverse array of products and services, from fashion to groceries and job listings to freelance gigs, we offer a platform for everyone. Our mission is to foster neighborhood bonds while providing affordable and accessible opportunities for sellers to thrive and buyers to discover local gems. Plus, we empower entrepreneurs by offering free creation of dynamic ecommerce websites. Join us in redefining the shopping experience, one neighborhood at a time, with NeighShop.
